silver_walkthrough.sol4

For the eyes of members of the Silver Bed only...

This document is regarding the title "The Möbius Strip" by the Round Eggs.

This document contains information about the project and a complete walkthrough of the game. Note that the walkthrough is purely the fastest way to finish the game. This cuts out any steps of the puzzle that give hints on how to succeed.

Enjoy...

-------------------------------------------

information(funFacts);

> The Möbius Strip is named after a symbol of the same name. This symbol represents infinity, and in some cases immortality.

> The design of Lawrence is based of the guy from the "that's how the mafia works" meme, Jovan is based off of Nicolas Cage, Robert is based off of Terry Hintz from LISA: The Painful, Samantha is based off of Manah from Drakengard, and Stallone... is based off of nothing. He's special that way.

> The name "Earl Celvar" comes from the color earl, which is grey, and his last name comes from a very poorly spelled silver.

> The text segments are inspired by the segments of NieR: Gestalt/RepliCant and NieR: Automata.

> The title screen and game icon is an infinity sign with a face. The right ring was at first an eyeball, but due to filters it became a blob and was never fixed.

-------------------------------------------

gameCompleted(supplementaryInfo);

Congratulations! You've now completed the entire game. If you enjoyed the plot, go to the website novella.garenburg.com. The site contains extra background, lore, and more info on the world that the Möbius Strip takes place in.

walkthrough(actOne, windows, mac, linux, html, mobile);

1. Interact with the LCD in Lab Room A. It's on the right of the room.

2. Interact with the mechanical piece next to a computer on the left of Lab Room B.

3. Interact with the computer in the middle of Lab Room B to get the final piece.

4. Go back to Lab Room A and interact with the computer on the left. Select "Create Machine," and then press OK.

5. MACHINE CREATION ORDER:

Extractor Shell

Memory Storage Unit

Soldering 1

Titanium Wire

Soldering 2

Solstice LCD

Soldering 3

6. Go to the testing room.

-------------------------------------------

walkthrough(actTwo, windows, mac, linux);

1. Interact with the computer on the immediate left of Lab Room B. Download the file.

2. Open the text file "authority_level" and replace the numbers with 1203 and save the file.

3. Go to the top corner of the room in the left (yes, the tiny corner above a light) and then toggle Lab Room A twice.

4. Open the text file "authority_level" and replace the numbers with 5839 and save the file.

5. Enter Lab Room C, which is in the middle of Lab Room B. Interact with all the objects.

6. Go back to Lab Room A and interact with the computer on the left. Select "Create Machine," and then press OK.

7. MACHINE CREATION ORDER:

Data Extraction Tool

OMNIFRONT Chip

Memory Storage Unit

Soldering 1

Broken Machine Piece B

Broken Machine Piece C

Soldering 3

8. Go to the testing room.
